Alfredo Pasta

Description

This is a rich and creamy fettuccine Alfredo recipe that even beginner cooks can make. This a simple dish that only requires 5 ingredients. With only 15 minutes of prep time and 15 minutes of cook time for a total of 30 minutes.

Ingredients

  • Pasta (Fettuccine)
  • Butter
  • Heavy Cream
  • Seasonings (Salt, Pepper, Garlic Salt)
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Bring water to a boil in a medium sized pot.
  2. Add the pasta and let simmer for about 10-11 minutes until al dente. Or cook another minute or two for more tender pasta.
  3. Strain the pasta
  4. Melt the butter and cream together on the stove, season, and stir in the cheese.
  5. Toss the pasta in the cheese sauce.
  6. Enjoy!
