Mahi Mahi Tacos

Description

These Mahi-Mahi Tacos are simple to prepare and are just as good as you'll get anywhere else. These tacos are layered with flavor after flavor, making them really delicious. And they only take 30 minutes to make.

Ingredients

  • Mahi mahi fillets (can substitute with fresh tuna)
  • Chili powder
  • Ground cumin
  • Smoked Paprika
  • Onion Powder
  • Garlic Powder
  • Kosher Salt
  • Lime Juice
  • Olive Oil
  • Pepper
  • Corn or Flour tortillas
  • Lime (freshly sliced)
  • Cilantro for garnish (Optional)
  • Jalapenos for added heat (Optional)

Steps

  1. Make the marinade by mixing together in a medium bowl the chili powder, cumin, paprika, onion, and garlic powders, salt, pepper, lime juice, and olive oil.
  2. Add the mahi mahi and turn to coat. Let rest for 15 minutes.
  3. Heat your grill to high heat.
  4. Place fish in a grill pan or cast iron skillet. Or, thread each fish piece with a skewer. Discard the left-over marinade.
  5. Grill the fish, carefully turning once, until opaque and just cooked through, about 8 minutes total.
  6. Heat the tortillas directly on the grill rack, or place in aluminum foil, and heat on the grill.
  7. Place the cooked fish in the warmed tortilla and top with slaw, fresh cilantro (optional), sliced jalapenos, and hot sauce (also optional).
